## What This Is

An interactive web visualization of the **Five States framework** — a map of human consciousness developed over 15 years at the intersection of Vedic science, contemplative science, and neuroscience.

The five states describe where your awareness lives *relative to your experience* at any given moment. Not personality types. Not stages of development. Navigable terrain — where you actually are right now.

Click any state in the diagram to see how awareness, identity, and experience relate to each other in that location.

---

## The Five States

| # | State | What's happening |
|---|-------|-----------------|
| 01 | **Foundation** | Identity is inside the experience. Circumstances feel all-consuming. Awareness can't keep up. |
| 02 | **Source** | Continuity detectable underneath experience. Awareness that was always here. |
| 03 | **Witness** | Awareness beside experience. Identity is no longer confined to it. |
| 04 | **Heart** | Awareness remains beside experience under load. Dual awareness stabilizes. |
| 05 | **Integrated** | Awareness remains continuous across changing states. The whole map becomes home. |

## The Core Idea

Most frameworks ask *what are you* — your type, your trait, your stage.

This one asks *where are you* — right now, in this moment, relative to what you're experiencing.

That's a different question. And it has a different kind of answer.

> *The state is not the self.*
